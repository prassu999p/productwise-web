Make informed choices about your food

ProductWise helps you understand what's in your food by scanning product labels and providing easy-to-understand nutritional information.

Features

Safety First

Identify potential allergens and harmful ingredients in your food products instantly.

Smart Analysis

Get AI-powered insights about the nutritional value and health impact of your food choices.

Scan History

Keep track of all your previous scans and build a personal database of products you consume.

Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored suggestions for healthier alternatives based on your preferences.

How It Works

1

Take a Photo

Take a clear photo of both the ingredients list and nutrition facts panel - you can capture them in one picture or take separate shots.

2

Instant Analysis

Our AI analyzes the ingredients and nutrition information to provide you with a comprehensive health assessment.

3

Review Results

Get a detailed breakdown of the product's health score, potential warnings, benefits, and personalized recommendations.
